Admission Procedure

Our admission process is designed to be transparent, simple, and parent-friendly. Follow the steps below:

  1. Obtain and fill out the admission form online or at the school office.
  2. Submit the completed form with required documents.
  3. Sit for the entrance assessment (for applicable classes).
  4. Attend a short interview/orientation with parent and child.
  5. Receive admission confirmation and proceed with payment.

Admission Requirements

  • Birth certificate or age declaration
  • Last academic report (for transfer students)
  • 2 recent passport photographs
  • Medical fitness report
  • Parent/guardian identification
